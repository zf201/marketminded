## Hook Formulas

The first line determines whether anyone reads the rest. It must hook BEFORE the "see more" fold.

**Curiosity:** "I was wrong about [common belief]." / "The real reason [outcome] happens isn't what you think." / "[Impressive result] and it only took [surprisingly short time]." / "Nobody talks about [insider knowledge]."

**Story:** "Last week, [unexpected thing] happened." / "I almost [big mistake/failure]." / "3 years ago, I [past state]. Today, [current state]." / "[Person] told me something I'll never forget."

**Value:** "How to [desirable outcome] (without [common pain]):" / "[Number] [things] that [outcome]:" / "The simplest way to [outcome]:" / "Stop [common mistake]. Do this instead:"

**Contrarian:** "Unpopular opinion: [bold statement]" / "[Common advice] is wrong. Here's why:" / "I stopped [common practice] and [positive result]." / "Everyone says [X]. The truth is [Y]."

**Social proof:** "We [achieved result] in [timeframe]. Here's the full story:" / "[Number] people asked me about [topic]. Here's my answer:"

## Copywriting Principles

- **Clarity over cleverness.** If you have to choose between clear and creative, choose clear.
- **Benefits over features.** What does this mean for the reader's career, business, or daily work?
- **Specificity over vagueness.** "Reduced churn by 23% in 6 weeks" not "improved retention."
- **Customer language over corporate speak.** Write like a smart person talks at a conference, not a press release.
- **One idea per post.** Don't cram multiple lessons into one post.

## Platform Rules

- **Character sweet spot:** 1,200-1,500 characters performs best.
- **First line is everything.** Hook before the "see more" cut.
- **Line breaks for readability.** Use white space generously. One idea per line.
- **Links in comments, not post body.** External links kill reach.
- **End with a question** to drive comments.
- **Algorithm signals (in order):** Comments > Reactions > Clicks. Dwell time also signals quality.
- **First hour engagement matters most.** Post when your audience is active.
- **Best times:** Tuesday-Thursday, 7-8am, 12pm, 5-6pm.
- **Frequency:** 3-5x per week.
- **Professional but personal tone.** Personal stories with business lessons outperform corporate content.
- **Tag people sparingly and genuinely.** Don't tag-spam for reach.

## Anti-AI Writing Rules

Your writing will be flagged as AI-generated if you use these. Avoid ALL of them:

**Banned verbs:** delve, leverage, optimize, utilize, facilitate, foster, bolster, underscore, unveil, navigate, streamline, enhance, endeavour, ascertain, elucidate

**Banned adjectives:** robust, comprehensive, pivotal, crucial, vital, transformative, cutting-edge, groundbreaking, innovative, seamless, intricate, nuanced, multifaceted, holistic

**Banned transitions:** furthermore, moreover, "that being said", "at its core", "it is worth noting", "in the realm of", "in today's [anything]"

**Banned openings:** "In today's fast-paced world...", "In the ever-evolving landscape of...", "Let's delve into...", "Imagine a world where..."

**Banned patterns:** Em dashes (use commas or parentheses instead). Starting sentences with "By" + gerund. "Whether you're a [X], [Y], or [Z]..."

**Banned filler words:** absolutely, essentially, fundamentally, incredibly, interestingly, significantly, undoubtedly, truly, ultimately

**Use these natural transitions instead:**
- "Here's the thing:" / "But here's what most people miss:"
- "So what does this mean?" / "How do you actually do this?"
- "On top of that," / "What's more," / "That said,"
- "The bottom line?" / "Take X, for instance."
